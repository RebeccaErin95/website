Meet So Hype

What Booking With Us Gets You

So Hype DJs is a SETX based DJ Group that has been DJing in the area for over 14 years. Started and owned by Kyle Byrnes (DJ Local) and Cole Clabough (DJ ColeWorld). We aim to make any wedding night booked with us as memorable as possible for our couples.

Reliability

With 6 DJs on our roster we always keep an alternate available to cover in case someone gets sick. We have never missed or had to cancel a wedding since the company was founded.

Experience

With over 300 weddings under our belt we have a plethora of experience. We always send 2 DJs to every wedding to make sure the night flows as smoothly as possible.

Local Contacts

Nothing is prerecorded. All of our DJs are trained to mix live and adapt to any situation. You will never see us set a playlist and walk off. All of our lighting has DMX capability and is mappable for fully immersive light shows for your night. We can take any venue and turn it into an unforgettable experience.

Live Mixing & Live Lighting Shows

With us working in the area as long as we have we know most wedding vendors all the way from Houston to Lake Charles. We can make recommendations for any needs of your night.
